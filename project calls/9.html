Signs: The Secret Language of the Universe



Laura Lynee Jackson


Signs: The Secret Language of the Universe" by Laura Lynne Jackson is a book that explores the concept of receiving and interpreting signs from the universe, which Jackson believes are messages from the other side, often from loved ones who have passed away."Signs" is a book that aims to help readers open their minds to the possibility of communication from the other side. By sharing her experiences and those of others, Laura Lynne Jackson provides insights and practical tips for recognizing and understanding the signs that the universe might be sending. The book serves as both a guide and a source of inspiration for those seeking to connect with the spiritual realm and find meaning in their everyday lives.
