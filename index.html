    About us

    Dads Unity Hub is a community dedicated to providing support and resources to single fathers who are going through the challenging journey of raising children alone after the loss of a partner. Our platform enables single fathers to connect with each other, share experiences, find guidance, and easy access to a bunch of information they need. We also offer a variety of resources to help you navigate the path of single parenting, which include the following:
    Expert Advice - information on legal rights, child custody, and support services available to single fathers in South Africa
    Blog & Testimonials - to share personal stories from other dads and their children
    Dating Resources - to assist you with finding companionship or a partner in crime when you are ready
    Nanny's Profiles - to help you find a reliable child minder, housekeeper or reputable day cares.
    We believe that support should be accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. So, don't hesitate ! Sign Up and create your account right away.
